Who I am

Marketing & eCommerce Manager for Yucca Packaging

I help our brand grow in global markets.

I didn’t start in marketing right away, in fact I only started about 6 months ago (3 months officially on paper) when I was asked to lead the web project. I spent about 2 and a half years in sales here, learning how to really listen, earn trust, and uncover the real challenges behind what our customers say and where we could improve. That foundation shaped how I work today - strategic, customer-focused, and always looking at the bigger picture.

When I moved into marketing, it felt like everything clicked. Suddenly, I could take the knowledge from our customers, psychology and problem-solving from sales and use it to build campaigns, our brand, and experiences that people actually want to be part of. My role covers global brand strategy, marketing, digital marketing, eCommerce platforms, and digital leadership - and I love the challenge of keeping it all moving forward.

Marketing shouldn’t be noise, it should resonate and build connection. The work I do makes sure the brand stands out.

I bring the commercial focus of sales, the creativity of marketing, and the curiosity of someone always learning new tools, whether that’s data analytics, automation, or full-stack development. My goal is to help our business grow in a way that lasts - not just short-term wins, but sustainable innovation and impact.

That’s really the kind of work I want to build my career on, and I'm in it right now.
